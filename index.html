Why We Celebrate New year

Celebrating new year is letting go what’s bad happens in the past year and welcoming the new one with new hopes and new plannings. Things which are undone in past year are in process with new energy in new year. Celebrating new year is to boost up yourself for the coming year. It’s all about learning and growing. Wishing new year to your friends and family is giving them good wishes that this coming new year will be the best for them. Most of us give new year gifts to give good wishes to our loved ones.
